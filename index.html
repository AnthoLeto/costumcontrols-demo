Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring my own controller? ‚ñº
Yes, but you do not need to bring one for the molding. We have some!
How long does the entire process take? ‚ñº
The molding session takes about 5 minutes. After that, we'll need approximately 2 days to 3D print your custom shells and prepare your controller for pickup or shipping.
What's the difference between TPU and PETG materials? ‚ñº
TPU is a flexible, rubber-like material that provides excellent grip and shock absorption - perfect if you prefer a softer, more cushioned feel. PETG is more rigid and durable, offering a solid, traditional controller feel while still being custom-fitted to your hands. Both materials are high-quality and long-lasting.
Will this work with my current PS5 or Xbox controller? ‚ñº
Absolutely! Since we're only replacing the outer shell of your existing controller, all the internal electronics, haptic feedback, adaptive triggers, and functionality remain exactly the same. It works perfectly with every PS5 game.
Can I get replacements if my controller shell gets damaged? ‚ñº
Yes! We keep your hand molds on file, so you can always order replacement shells if needed. We also offer a warranty on manufacturing defects and provide lifetime support for all our customers.
Do you ship, or is it pickup only? ‚ñº
We offer both options! You can pick up your finished controller at any of our partner locations, or we can ship it to you anywhere in Canada. Shipping costs vary based on your location.

Choose Your Material

We offer two premium materials, each with unique benefits. Both are durable, high-quality, and custom-fitted to your hands.

TPU - Flexible & Grippy

Thermoplastic polyurethane provides a soft, rubber-like feel with excellent shock absorption and superior grip for intense gaming sessions.

PETG - Firm & Durable

Strong and impact-resistant material, offering a solid structure while remaining slightly flexible. Ideal for everyday gaming use.
